Rubi Slide

If you click on first column's top button. The first square will go down and the others square will climb of one block. And conversely, if you click on first column's bottom button. The last square will climb at top of the line and the others square will go down of one block. For lines is the same thing. If you click on first line's left button. The first square will go at end and others will be move on the left of one block. And if you click on the first line's right button, it is exactly the same thing but in the other direction.

The goal are to reproduce a pattern. To accomplish that, you can move each columns and each lines To start the game, you should click on shuffle's button. Like that, the game will be blend, the chronometer will start and you will be able to move the columns and the lines
